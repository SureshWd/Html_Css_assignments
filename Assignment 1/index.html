Second Blog:-

Tour of beautiful Varanasi

Varanasi, one of the world's oldest cities, is home of the sacred Kashi vishwanath Temple.Located on the bank of Ganges River.This is a religious temple of hindus. It attracts millions of devotees and tourists every year. Visiting during Maha Shivaratri can be a unique experience.It is not just a religious pilgrimage; it is a journey into the spiritual heart of India.
  1. We gain some spiritual experience here because of numerous ancient temples.
  2. Experience the beauty of the Ganges with a sunrise or sunset boat ride.
  3. Visit some historical landmarks like Sarnath, where buddha gave his first sermon.
  4. Enjoy delicious street food and explore local markets for Banarasi silk and handicrafts.
Varansi, also known as Banaras. Here are some additional attractions, like:- Sarnath, Banaras Hindu University(BHU), Ganga Aarti at Dashashwamedh Ghat and Local cuisines etc.

Third Blog:-

A religious tour of Ayodhya

Ayodhya is a city that holds immense historical and religious significance. It is located at the bank of Sarayu River. It is Known as the birthplace of lord Rama so it is a religious destination for millions of Hindus. Hanuman Garhi is another major temple, dedicated to lord Hanuman. Ayodhya's history is as rich as its spiritual heritage.
  1. Visit the spiritual sites like:- Ram Temple, Hanuman Garhi, and Nageshwarnath temple etc.
  2. Explore Kanak Bhawan and Treta ke Thakur for historical and architectural significance.
  3. Enjoy the peaceful beauty and atmosphere of the Sarayu riverfront.
  4. Best time to visit here is October to March Month for pleasant weather.
Ayodhya offers a rich spirituality and history with key sites like:- Ram Janmabhoomi ,Sita Rasoi, Kanak Bhawan etc. Ayodhya is a beautiful place to travel for enjoyment and also religious purpose.

Fourth Blog:-

Solo trip to Kashmir

Kashmir is located in the northern part of India is a destination of breathtaking landscapes, vibrant culture and peaceful beauty. Srinagar is the main gateway, well-connected by air and road. The nearest railway station is in Jammu. Kashmir is a destination that promises an unforgettable experience with its stunning landscapes and adventures activities.
  1. Visit Srinagar to see Dal lake and Nigeen lake where we can enjoy a peaceful shikara ride.
  2. For adventures things like:- skiing, snowboarding, and gondola rides etc. visit Gulmarg
  3. Also visit some historical places like:- Shankaracharya Temple, hazratbal Shrine and Jamia Masjid etc.
  4. Visit Kashmir to see its cultural richness like:- Local cuisines , handicrafts etc.
Kashmir known as "Paradise on Earth," offers stunning landscapes,adventures activities and rich cultural experiences. Plan your trip to this paradise and immerse yourself in its timeless beauty.
